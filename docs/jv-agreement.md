# Joint Venture Agreement Outline

A comprehensive outline covering all aspects of a real estate joint venture agreement. This was the initial framework document.

> This is a draft outline for discussion purposes. It must be reviewed by a licensed real estate attorney before use.

## Sections Covered

1. **Initial Capital Contributions & Ownership Structure** -- Three options (Tiered Equity, 50/50 + Priority Return, Gradual Vesting)
2. **Ongoing Financial Obligations** -- Monthly payment splits, expense tracking
3. **Rental Income Distribution** -- Three approaches for splitting rental income
4. **Appreciation & Depreciation Handling** -- Annual valuation methods
5. **Buyout & Exit Provisions** -- Right of first refusal, fair market value, forced sale
6. **Default & Inability to Pay** -- Grace periods, equity adjustments, hardship provisions
7. **Death, Disability & Bankruptcy** -- Life insurance, estate handling, divorce clauses
8. **Major Repairs & Capital Improvements** -- Decision thresholds, emergency repairs, improvement credits
9. **Management & Decision-Making** -- Co-management, designated manager, or third-party options
10. **Dispute Resolution** -- Negotiation, mediation, arbitration, litigation
11. **Tax Considerations** -- LLC structure, tax allocations, capital gains
12. **Additional Provisions** -- Books/records, insurance, amendments, governing law

For the full text of each section, see the [complete JV Agreement Outline document](https://github.com).
