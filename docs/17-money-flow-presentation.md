## The Monthly Payment Breakdown

### Total Monthly Payment: $3,090.10

| Component | Amount | % of Payment | Where It Goes |
|-----------|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **Principal** | $285.51 | 9.2% | Builds YOUR equity |
| **Interest** | $2,385.46 | 77.2% | Bank's profit |
| **Property Tax** | $307.41 | 10.0% | Government |
| **Insurance** | $111.72 | 3.6% | Insurance company |

> **Only 9.2% of your payment builds equity. The rest leaves the system.**

## 5-Year Cumulative Money Flow

| Destination | Annual | 5-Year Total | Stays? |
|-------------|--------|--------------|--------|
| Principal | $3,426 | $17,130 | YES |
| Interest | $28,626 | $143,128 | NO |
| Property Tax | $3,689 | $18,444 | NO |

```
Every year, $33,655 LEAVES the system.
Only $3,426 STAYS as equity.

That's 90.8% gone, 9.2% kept.
```
