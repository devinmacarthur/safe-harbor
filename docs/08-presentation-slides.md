# Presentation Slides: Safe Harbor Agreement

Use this document to build your PowerPoint presentation. Each section = one slide. Speaker notes are included for what to say.

---

## SECTION 1: OPENING

### Slide 1: Title Slide

```
SAFE HARBOR
A Fair Co-Ownership Agreement for Our Home

Mae & Starseed
1708 NE 106th Avenue, Portland

January 2026
```

**Speaker Notes:**
"I put this together because I think it's important we get on the same page about how our home ownership works -- not because anything is wrong, but because being clear now protects our friendship later."

---

### Slide 2: Why Are We Here?

```
We bought a house together. We never wrote down:

  - How do we split equity if we sell?
  - What if one of us wants out?
  - What if the market goes up or down?
  - How do we account for different contributions?

Let's figure it out now -- while everything is good.
```

**Speaker Notes:**
"When we bought the place, we were focused on getting the deal done. Totally normal. But we have different types of contributions -- your parents' generous down payment, and our ongoing mortgage payments. We should agree on how that translates to ownership."

---

### Slide 3: What This Is (and Isn't)

```
This IS:
  - A framework for fairness
  - Protection for everyone involved
  - A conversation starter

This is NOT:
  - Legal advice
  - A final contract
  - Me trying to "take" anything
```
