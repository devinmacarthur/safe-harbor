# Grant Funding Strategy for the Lot Split

How to maximize grant funding tied to Cascadia AI Systems Lab operations on the L-shaped parcel.

> **Note:** Grant programs change frequently. Verify deadlines, eligibility, and availability before applying. This document reflects research as of March 2026.

---

## Two Layers of Funding

1. **Property & site development** — Grants that offset the cost of developing the parcel itself
2. **Business operations & research** — Federal/state grants that fund Cascadia's work and justify having a fixed base

---

## Tier 1: Property & Site Development Funding

### The Zoning Problem

Most Portland property development grants require **commercial or mixed-use zoning**, not R7 residential. This creates a strategic decision:

**Option A: Rezone after partition**
- After the lot split, apply to rezone Starseed's parcel from R7 to CR (Commercial Residential)
- CR is specifically designed for "small and isolated sites in residential neighborhoods"
- Unlocks Prosper Portland property grants AND increases land value
- Timeline: 6-12 months; cost: a few thousand in application fees

**Option B: Operate as home-based business**
- Portland R7 allows home occupations under Title 33.203
- Can operate Cascadia from the parcel without rezoning
- Won't qualify for most commercial property grants
- Still qualifies for all federal research/business grants

**Recommendation:** Start with Option B to get operational immediately, then pursue Option A once the partition is complete.

### Portland/Oregon Property Programs

| Program | Amount | What It Covers | Key Requirement |
|---------|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Prosper Portland Local Repair Grant** | Varies | Facility repairs, renovations, improvements | Must be a business location |
| **Prosper Portland Commercial Property Loan** | $25K-$500K (predevelopment) | Soft costs for exploring a real estate project | Commercial/employment/industrial zoning |
| **Prosper Portland Affordable Commercial Tenanting** | Up to $300K | Tenant improvements, permitting, space readiness | Commercial-zoned space |
| **Oregon Business Development Fund (OBDF)** | Gap financing | Land, buildings, equipment, machinery | Must work with a traditional lender |

These become available after rezoning to CR or CM1.

## Priority Action Plan

### Immediate (March 2026)

1. **Register with SAM.gov** — Required for all federal grants and contracts
2. **Get a UEI number** for Cascadia AI Systems Lab (replaces DUNS)
3. **Contact USGS Small Business Office** — Register capabilities for Earth MRI subcontracts
4. **Watch for NSF Tech Labs solicitation** — Expected Spring 2026

### Q2 2026

5. **Contact WA and OR state geological surveys** — Earth MRI subcontracting opportunities
6. **Contact WSDOT** — Register as small-business subcontractor for Cascadia HSR corridor work
7. **Monitor sbir.gov weekly** — SBIR/STTR reauthorization
8. **Identify NSERC Alliance partner** at a Canadian university

### After Lot Partition Completes

9. **Apply to rezone parcel** from R7 to CR (Commercial Residential)
10. **Once rezoned, apply for Prosper Portland property improvement grants**
11. **Apply for OBDF gap financing** for any structures/infrastructure on the parcel

### Ongoing

12. File BC METC and federal CMETC claims for all qualifying survey expenses
13. Track all property development costs as potential business deductions (consult CPA)
14. Attend Prosper Portland Office of Small Business community office hours

## Biggest Unlocks

1. **NSF Tech Labs** — Brand new program explicitly for independent research organizations. Multi-year awards, significant funding. Cascadia fits the profile perfectly.
2. **USGS Earth MRI subcontracts** — Cascadia's core service (drone magnetometry) is exactly what they procure. $129M spent on data acquisition since 2019.
3. **Rezoning to CR** — Opens the door to Prosper Portland property grants ($25K-$300K range) and increases land value.
4. **SBIR/STTR reauthorization** — When it returns, multiple agencies fund exactly what Cascadia does. Phase I alone is $150K-$275K.
